Updated: IPTV Tube now support m3u playlist, Goto Menu > Local M3U/M3U8
INFO: To report a bug, please contact me via Telegram. https://t.me/arma7x
1) Goto Menu > Clear Local Caches to remove old local caches
2) The Loading Indicator on top indicate the player is buffering the data from iptv server
3) The Loading Indicator on top indicate the player is buffering the data from iptv server
4) If the Loading Indicator keep buffering but won't playing the audio/video, probably the channel is not available on your region
5) Use Arrow Up/Down adjust volume
6) IPTV Tube cache the playlist on your default storage
7) M3U: must be a valid playlist file and can store many links
8) Setup M3U
  • 8.1) Goto Menu > Local M3U/M3U8 to play your local m3u files. Click Refresh to reload all local m3u files inside local storages
9) M3U8: can store single m3u8 link
10) Setup M3U8
  • 10.1) Paste your m3u8 link inside text file. The link must be a valid URL, such as https://test-streams.mux.dev/x36xhzz/x36xhzz.m3u8
  • 10.2) Rename the previous text file by your preferred filename, such as Big Buck Bunny.iptv. The file extension is .iptv
  • 10.3) Copy your text file(Big Buck Bunny.iptv) to internal or external storage
  • 10.4) Goto Menu > Local M3U/M3U8 to play your local m3u8 files. Click Refresh to reload all local m3u8 files inside local storages
11) Goto Menu > Clear Local Caches to clear caches on local storages
