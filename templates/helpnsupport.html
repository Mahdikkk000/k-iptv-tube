1) Some 1080p video won't playing the audio/video properly due to codec issues
2) The Loading Indicator on top indicate the player is buffering the data from iptv server
3) If the Loading Indicator keep buffering but won't playing the audio/video, probably the channel is not available on your region
4) Use Arrow Up/Down adjust volume
5) IPTV Tube cache the playlist on your default storage
6) Local M3U8(new)
  • 6.1) To create your m3u8 file, paste your m3u8 URL inside text file. The URL must be a valid link, such as https://test-streams.mux.dev/x36xhzz/x36xhzz.m3u8
  • 6.2) Rename the text file by your preferred filename, such as Big Buck Bunny.iptv
  • 6.3) Copy your m3u8 file(Big Buck Bunny.iptv) to internal or external storage
  • 6.4) Goto Menu > Local M3U8 to play your local m3u8 files. Click Refresh to reload new m3u8 files inside storage
  • 6.5) The file extension is iptv
  • 6.6) Each file must contain only single m3u8 URL
